Welcome, I’m Wayan.

I’ve been a chef for over 25 years, and can’t count the number of times someone has asked me for the recipes to their favorite dishes I’ve made. So, I created this website to share some of those recipes. But first, a little bit about me.

I was born and raised in Bali, Indonesia, where my passion for food was ignited my mother’s cooking. In 1997, I graduated from the Epicurean School of Culinary Arts in Los Angeles, and have worked in catering, fine restaurants, hotels, and country clubs ever since. I am currently the Executive Chef at a prestigious country club on the East Coast of the U.S.

I created and coded this website myself, and am still working to make it a better experience for you. So for now, enjoy it and have fun! I hope my recipes boost your confidence as a chef, whether you’re cooking for yourself at home or for customers at a restaurant.
